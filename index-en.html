About Me

I am Maurits and I help organizations solve societal and business challenges. I don't position myself as a traditional consultant, but as an active participant who dares to dive into the deep end.

My approach at o8o is simple: less talk, more action. I specialize in turning vague ideas into plans that people can actually work with in practice. I bring the energy and methodology to let groups shift gears faster.

With a strong network at Erasmus University, TU Delft, and the Rotterdam SME sector, I connect knowledge with execution. From boardroom strategy meetings to complex societal transitions: I provide the structure and pace needed for real change.
